Airports

There are two airports convenient to San Francisco.

  • San Francisco International (SFO)
  • Oakland International (OAK)

It's easy to get into San Francisco from either airport, but it's best to avoid taking a taxi since it's a long expensive ride. An airport shuttle is your best bet or you can take the BART train to Powell Street. There is a BART station in the San Francisco airport. At the Oakland airport there is a shuttle bus that goes to the Oakland Coliseum station, where you can catch the BART train for Powell St. The fare is $5.35 one way from SFO or $3.55 from Oakland.
