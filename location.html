Where is the wedding?

We are having the ceremony and reception at the Trocadero Clubhouse in Sigmund Stern Grove. The clubhouse is nestled in the woods in the South East most corner of the park, on the corner of 19th Avenue and Sloat Boulevard. The entrance to Stern Grove is located on Sloat Boulevard. There is very limited parking at the clubhouse itself. Some street parking is available on Sloat but it is a long walk down a steep hill and we encourage everyone to take advantage of the shuttle that we will be providing from the Westin St. Francis hotel.

From the North Bay: Golden Gate Bridge to 19th Avenue/Park Presidio exit. Continue on 19th Avenue. Turn right at Sloat Boulevard.

From the South Bay: 280-North to 19th Avenue exit. At the first stoplight, bear right onto Junipero Serra. Turn left at Sloat Boulevard.

From the East Bay: Bay Bridge to 101-South to 280-South. Exit at Ocean Avenue and turn right. Bear right onto Junipero Serra. Turn left at Sloat Boulevard.
